H E N G E is a virtual ritual site that lives in the digital desert, laying dormant in total darkness, and only seen once heard. First a witness, you become an accomplice to its circular ceremony through rhythmic sculpting and textural mixing. Suddenly hordes of invisible hands past and present reveal their sonic marks, as H E N G E relays each visitors' touches to one another.

A surrogate space for tactile intimacy, H E N G E indulges our currently stifled pre-linguistic urges to commune and attune.
